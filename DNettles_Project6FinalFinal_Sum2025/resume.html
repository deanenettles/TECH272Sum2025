Resume

Deane Nettles is a graphic designer, photographer, and illustrator. He has an MFA in Integrated Design from the University of Baltimore and has taught at numerous colleges and universities in the DC/Baltimore area.

He began on a college newspaper as a photographer and learned production and designing on the grid. He received his undergraduate degree in news journalism. Then he went back for a degree in advertising design while editing the same student paper. The former editor of the newspaper got him a position working as a production artist at boutique agency Morgan Burchette; from there he became art director at KSK Communications.

Since then he has freelanced for ad agencies, design studios and direct marketing firms. He has been art director at several publications and senior art director at August, Lang & Husak, who he still manages projects for.

He is a past president and life member of the Art Directors Club of Metropolitan Washington where he ran the Annual Show and produced programs and workshops.

He has been teaching since 2006 and has taught courses in design, illustration and web design and advanced courses in typography, advertising and branding.

Deane is always looking to work with an energetic, committed team like the one on his college newspaper — teams told to quiet down by the classes next door and happy to continue their enthusiastic arguments over pizzas after hours.

In addition he is currently learning type design and javascript; is married with a wife, a dog and a cat; draws, paints and sculpts; bikes over a thousand miles a year; and dances swing, waltz, blues and zydeco.
